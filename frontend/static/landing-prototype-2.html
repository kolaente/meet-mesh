Features

Two Powerful Tools. One Platform.

Everything you need to schedule meetings, your way.

Booking Links

Real-Time Availability

Syncs with your CalDAV calendar to show only times you're actually free. No double bookings, ever.

Shareable Links

Custom URLs for each booking type. Share via email, embed on your website, or add to your signature.

Email Confirmations

Automatic emails with calendar invites. Guests can reschedule or cancel with one click.

Group Polls

Multi-Person Coordination

Perfect for team meetings, events, or any gathering. Everyone votes on times that work for them.

Simple Voting

Yes/No/If-Needed voting makes it easy to find consensus. See at a glance which times work best.

Final Selection

Lock in the winning time and notify all participants. Calendar invites sent automatically.

Self-Hosted

Your Data. Your Server. Your Rules.

Meet Mesh runs entirely on your infrastructure. No third-party services, no data sharing, no surprises.

Complete Privacy

Your calendar data never leaves your server. Full GDPR compliance out of the box.

Full Control

Configure OIDC, SMTP, and CalDAV to your exact needs. Integrate with your existing stack.

Single Binary

One executable, one config file. Deploy anywhere in minutes with Docker or bare metal.
